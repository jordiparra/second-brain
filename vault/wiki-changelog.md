# Wiki changelog

Append-only record of wiki changes. Newest-first — new entries are prepended to the top. Existing entries are never modified.

Format: one paragraph per entry, blank line between entries. Lead with the date in bold, an em dash, then a capitalized verb (Ingested, Updated, Added, Fixed, Renamed, Created) describing what changed and why.

**YYYY-MM-DD** — Capitalized verb describing what changed and why.
